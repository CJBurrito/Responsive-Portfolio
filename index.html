About Me

Hi, I'm Christian, a 24 year old living in Charlotte, NC. I currently work in the accounting department at AmWINS in Southpark, an insurance wholesaler. My free time is spent making music and jamming with friends, and playing video games every so often.


I was born in Long Beach, CA, and lived in the LA basin for the first 21 years of my life. The move to North Carolina was instigated by the purchase of the company I worked for at the time. Our purchaser had their accounting headquarters in Charlotte, and my family decided we'd try and take the plung and move across the country.

I am currently taking a coding bootcamp class at UNC Charlotte to get into a new carrier and possibly find something that I really truly love.
